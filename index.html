Jacob Pomerantz

Hello! My name is Jacob Pomerantz. I am a second-year mechanical engineering student at Northwestern with an interest in manufacturing and design. I like to spend my time with others, whether it be on a mountain skiing or hiking, or in a lab working on projects. I have a passion for helping others. I am currently leading a team designing a machine that manufactures HIV implant medication. I am excited about the potential this machine has to help people. People have called me a granola iron man, but don’t let that intimidate you. Just kidding, no one calls me that. Feel free to view my work below. Enjoy!
